Update Log — v0.1.1

Balance

  • Archers: damage & cadence changed from 15 / 0.5s → 10 / 0.75s. Range remains 120.

Bug Fixes

  • Units no longer drift toward center when the king isn’t activated; lane-local defense only after activation.
  • River is a strict no-go (clamped to bridge corridors); no clipping while defending near king towers.
  • AI obeys bridge rules and uses a 2-card rotation like the player.

Misc

  • Added placement tiles (green = valid, red = invalid) visible when a card is selected.
  • Elixir bar is now segmented into 10 ticks for clearer refills.
