Gameplay / Pathing

  • Three-phase movement: toBridge → crossBridge → attack.
  • Lane-biased A* (corridor cells are cheaper); no cross-river aggro.
  • Aggro to nearby enemies only if same side & same lane.
  • Light separation steering to reduce bunching at bridge exits.
